Laptop Buying Guide For CS Student

Author - Abhishek
27 July 2021. 4 min read

Laptop is very essential for students nowdays and for CS student it is must. This article is publishing on july 2021 so I will first tell you some requiremnts for laptop and this is futureproof also.

Reqiurements

Processor

Intel Core i5 11th Generation or AMD Ryzen 5 Series (example- 5500U)

RAM

Minimum 8gb (must be upgradable)

Display Resolution

Atleast FULL HD 1920X1080

And there are many types of display available but I would Suggest you for go to IPS display. Because it shows true colors.

Storage

SSD are way faster than HDD.

So, go for 512 GB NVME M.2 SSD.

AND 256 GB NVME M.2 SSD (only if you are low on budget).

Must have an option for upgradation so you can put another ssd.

Graphic Card

For programming there is no need of graphic card.

And Intel Iris or Amd Radeon can easily help you to play games on medium or low settings,

But if your main purpose of buying this laptop is to play games then choose from Nvdia GTX 1650 to RTX 3080.
